The Run Around( Brand Race)- Racing on Empty

First Price: ₹
Registration Fee: ₹

It is a competition where students get a chance to imagine and introduce a new brand with recyclable or reusable products. The challenge for the students is to make a new product and design a marketing strategy that will successfully create an image of the new brand in the minds of the customers.
Round 1:

  • All the participants have to email their PowerPoint presentations.
Round 2:
  • The selected participants will have to present their PPT in front of an esteemed panel of judges.
  • The participants should complete their presentations in 5-7 minutes.
  • The judges can ask questions if they want further insight on any concept.
  • The winner will be selected based on the judges' score.

Rules for the Event:

  • It is a group event. It is mandatory to make a product and create a name, logo & tagline for the new brand.
  • Participants are not allowed to use an existing brand or logo. It'll lead to direct disqualification.
  • Product must be exclusively comprised of fully recycled or reused material.
  • The presentation in Round 2 should not exceed 7 minutes per participant.
  • A maximum of 10 slides is allowed in the PowerPoint presentation.
  • Participants will be judged on their creativity, innovation, logic, and presentation skills.
