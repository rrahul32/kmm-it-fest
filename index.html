QUIZ BUCKERS (Business Quiz)

First Prize: ₹4001
Second Prize: ₹2501
Registration Fee: ₹200
Date: 06-02-2023
Spot Reg Time: 8:30 - 10:30 AM

Guidelines:

  • Participation in the form of team of two members only.
  • Quiz will have questions related to the world of business.
  • There are no restrictions on the number of teams from an institution.
  • In case of a tie during the qualifying written round between teams, they would be required to answer 5 more questions (written mode) within 5 mins.
  • Top five teams qualifying in the Written round, shall proceed to the next round.
  • Quiz will commence with qualifying written round followed by subsequent rounds as mentioned below.
      Round 1
      Round 2
      Round 3
      Round 4
  • The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or any electronic device, during any stages of the Quiz.
  • The decision of the Quiz master and the Judges will be final and binding.

QUALIFYING ROUND

25 questions (objectives) will be given to each team. Time provided will be 15 minutes
ROUND-1 (Written test)
  • Each team will be given 15 questions(objective).
  • Questions should be answered within 10 minutes.
  • Lowest scorer team will be eliminated.
ROUND -2
  • Each Team will be asked 5 questions.
  • Each question will carry 10 marks.
  • Four options will be given for each question.
  • 60 seconds will be given to answer each question.
  • This is not a passable round.
  • No Negative marks for wrong answers.
  • Lowest scorer team will be eliminated .
ROUND -3
  • In this round 3 pictures will be shown to each team.
  • They have to identify the personality/logo/product within 30 seconds.
  • This is a passable round and carries negative marking.
  • Passed question should be answered within 10 seconds.
  • Each correct answer carries 10 marks and for wrong answers 5 marks will be deducted.
  • Bonus 5 marks will be given to the team for each right answer to the passed question and 3 marks will be deducted for each wrong answer to the passed question.
  • Lowest scorer team will be eliminated.
ROUND-4
  • This is a rapid fire round.
  • Each team will be asked 10 questions.
  • 10 seconds will be given for answering each question.
  • Each question carries 10 marks.
  • If the team does not want to answer a question, it has to loudly say “PASS” and the next question will be asked.
  • This is not a passable round and there is no negative marking.
  • Questions once asked won't be repeated.
  • The Team which has secured the highest mark in this round will be declared as the “WINNER”.
    • Note*: In case of a tie between Teams, Round- 5 will commence to choose the Winner.
ROUND -5 (If Required…)
  • In this round, 5 questions will be asked to each team.
  • It is a rapid fire round and 10 Secs will be given for answering each question.

Track, Trail & Trail

Register Now

KMM College of Arts and Science Thrikkakara, Ernakulam is pleased to invite students compete in Track, Trail & Trail.

Treasure hunt, the idea of hunting for hidden treasures has always been exciting and adventurous. In treasure hunt, participants work in small teams to find the treasure with the help of given clues. The team who decodes the clue and finds the treasure first will win the game. The game of treasure hunt comes from the concept of scavenger hunting where participants have to find number of items from the list provided to them. Treasure hunt differentiates itself from scavenger hunting by providing given clues to find the treasure. In this digital era, treasure hunt games are organized online with the use of websites and social media handles. The first Internet scavenger hunt was organized by Rick Gates in 1992. Since KMM College of Arts and Science is organizing this event, the clues will be given.

ELIGIBILITY

  • Participants from all backgrounds, genders are welcome.
  • Participants should report in time with their id cards.
  • Maximum 5 members/minimum 3 members in a team.

REGISTRATION

  • Registration Fee costs 1000 per team.
  • All applicants must register by midnight of 4th Feb 2023.
  • Spot registration is permitted for those who can't register online.
  • Spot Reg. Time: 8:30 - 10:30 AM
  • Each applicant must enter the required information on the registration form.
  • No late submission will be submitted and any incomplete, false, fraudulent or tampered forms will not be considered.

CONCEPT OF EVENT

  • Each team can have minimum of 3 members or maximum 5 members.
  • Each team will get a clue related to the treasure.
  • The clues will vary for each team.
  • The winning team will get a sum of 10k.
  • No runner ups.
  • Event won't be conducted if there are less than 5 groups registered.

PROCESS OF SELECTION

  • There will be total of 3 rounds.
  • The 1st 6 team to solve the 1st round will be qualified for 2nd round.
  • The 1st and 2nd round consist of same amounts of clues to be solved in same amount of time.
  • In 2nd round 1st 2 teams to solve the clues will be qualified to the final round.
  • The clues for the last round will be images and it should be solved in limited time.
  • The difficulty level of the clues will increase with each round.

SCHEDULE OF THE EVENT

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA

    The judgement will be done on the basis of the time taken by the teams.
